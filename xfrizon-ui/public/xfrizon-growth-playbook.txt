Xfrizon Growth Playbook (Top 10)

1. Programmatic SEO pages
Create indexable pages for city, genre, and organizer intent with unique text.

2. Event + list schema
Use Event schema on detail pages and ItemList schema on listing pages.

3. Social-first share assets
Generate event/blog share images with date, city, CTA, and brand mark.

4. Referral loop
Give users personal referral links and reward conversions.

5. Organizer creator toolkit
Provide captions, hashtags, and ready-made story/post assets.

6. Sticky conversion CTAs
Keep Buy Tickets visible on mobile to reduce checkout drop-off.

7. Lifecycle messaging
Automate alerts for new events, low ticket inventory, and recaps.

8. Content flywheel
Publish recurring city-based roundup content and interlink deeply.

9. Fast analytics loop
Track share clicks, referral conversion, and save-to-purchase rates.

10. SSR/prerender for key pages
Render public pages server-side or prerender for stronger indexing.
